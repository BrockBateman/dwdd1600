History

The First Generation!

The first ever games to be made in the Pokemon franchise is Pokemon Red/Blue/Green, and later on Pokemon Yellow edition. Pokemon Red and Green where released in Japan in 1996. Pokemon Red and Blue released in internationally in 1998, and during this time The Pokemon Company decided to expand into creating a trading card game, a manga/comic book and an anime/tv show. Trading card game's first set was released in Oct 1996, and the manga was first released in Mar 1997 in Japan. The first season of the anime was released in Apr 1997. All of these installments get many more additions to them, the games get 3 spin-off games, and a crossover with super smash bros64. The trading card game get's its own video game version. The anime gets another season, and 2 movies. This spans from the years 1996-1999.

Continuing path of The Pokemon Company!

After the first generaton, the Pokemon compnay goes onto make many more games, movies, seasons for the anime, more trading card game sets, and more installments to the manga too! At the time of the creation of this page there are currently

  • Nine generations of pokemon games.
  • 25 seasons of the pokemon anime.
  • 98 card sets released in America, 91 in Japan.

All of these will be continuing within the year, and years to come. It has become an international phenomenon, that spans all ages.
